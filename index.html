Welcome to UW's Neurotech RSO

We're a neurotech RSO at UW. Synaptech is a club that looks to introduce people of all disciplines into the field of neural engineering. We do a lot of really cool projects ranging from hardware hacking to developing software for neurotech hardware (and maybe even a bit of VR). If you're looking for somewhere to work on personal projects, this is the place! All experience levels are welcome.
